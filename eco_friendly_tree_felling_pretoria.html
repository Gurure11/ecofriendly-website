About Us

Over ten years of practical outdoor service experience in Pretoria.

Eco Friendly Tree Felling Pretoria is a Pretoria-based outdoor service team focused on safe tree work, cleaner properties and attractive gardens. We work with homeowners, complexes, rental properties and businesses that need reliable help with tree felling, stump clearing, refuse removal and garden maintenance.

Our approach is built around careful planning, tidy execution and a strong respect for the environment around every site. We aim to reduce unnecessary damage, separate green waste where possible, and leave spaces safer and easier to use after the job is done. Whether a client needs one dangerous tree removed, a neglected yard restored, or routine gardening support, we deliver practical solutions backed by workmanship and responsive service.

Eco-minded service: sensible waste handling, neat site clean-up and a greener mindset on every job.
Safety first: especially important for trees near walls, roofs, driveways, fences or power-sensitive areas.
Detail focused: we do not just cut and leave — we aim to restore order and improve the look of your property.
